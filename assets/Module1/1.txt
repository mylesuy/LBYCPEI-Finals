WHAT ARE ONLINE ETIQUETTES?

Be Respectful
–If you can’t say it in front of someone,
  then don’t say it online.
Check what you have written
–Make sure it is not rude
  or may cause misunderstandings.
Be careful with humor and sarcasm
–Ask yourself: “Would everyone get the joke?”.
Check the privacy settings of your post
–You don’t want strangers seeing your posts or
  knowing where you live.
Check your requests
–Make sure you know or recognize the person
  that is requesting a friend request or
  a group invite.
Read the rules, guidelines, and policies
–Be familiar with the rules of the network,
  so everyone would have a good online experience.
