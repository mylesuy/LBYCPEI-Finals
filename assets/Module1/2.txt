Proofread before posting
–Be professional and formal when writing anything
  online and also check your spellings.
Cite your sources
–Credit the original creator of the content that
  you are sharing.
Don’t post or share inappropriate materials
–Keep it children-friendly, the internet is accessible
  to everyone, including kids.
Be forgiving
–Be understanding of others, some people aren’t
  familiar with the rules yet or aren’t good
  in writing things.
