HOW DO I DISTINGUISH FAKE NEWS AND REAL
NEWS?

Develop a Critical Mindset
–Approach everything in a rational and critical manner.
Check the source
–Make sure the source is credible and up to date.
Find more information or articles about the story
–Make sure you know more about the story and
  know all sides of the story.
Examine the Claims or Evidence
–Make sure that the claims in the story actually
  proves that something definitely happened.
Don’t easily believe at the Images
–Some images are reused from an old post or
  might be edited.
Use your common sense
–Fake news are designed to make people easily
  believe something and cause misunderstandings.
