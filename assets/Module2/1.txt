WHAT IS CYBERCRIME?
–It is type of crime, where a computer is involved or 
  is used to commit a crime.

TYPES OF CYBERCRIME:
Identity Theft
–Occurs when a criminal or hacker gains access and
  steal someone’s personal data.
Phishing
–A type of attack, where the hacker creates a fake
  website or email that is imitating a legitimate
  company to trick unsuspecting victim.
Ransomware
–A program that commonly encrypts a victim’s data,
  and demands a ransom to decrypt and return the victim’s data.
Malware
–A program that is used to spy a person and also to
  steal their information, like logging a person’s
  login passwords, spying a person’s online
  activities, steal personal information, etc.
