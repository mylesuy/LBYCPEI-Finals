How can I protect myself online?

Check the connection & make sure it’s secured
–Web Browsers shows a padlock icon beside the URL
  if the connection is secured.

Keep your confidential information to yourself.

Companies and banks would never ask for your
  personal information or accounts. In case there are 
  issues regarding your account, they would always ask
  you to go to their office or branch.

Comapnies would not send emails regarding account
  recovery or any account-related transactions (ex:
  Password Change / Recovery), they will always ask
  you to go to their office/branch or go to their official
  website.
